Welcome to Let's Do This Fitness

We are an “In-Studio” LesMills Virtual group fitness facility, also offering the best live Zumba classes in the Denver metropolitan area! Our “In-Studio” Virtual Group Fitness concept and Extended Hour Access is the first of its kind in Colorado. Our studio has a communal feel and encourage Members to workout TOGETHER!!! Extended Hour Access (EHA) from 5a to 11:59p makes coordinating workouts with others easy and convenient. The Let’s Do This Fitness APP allows Members to reserve studios for 30 and 60 minute workouts with studio keycard access. Workout on your schedule with the best LesMills National Presenters/Trainers on a huge Video Wall. Use LesMillsSmartbars, SmartSteps, and SmartBand equipment, and enjoy concert lighting and a booming sound system. Positive energy, like-minded peers, and a fun atmosphere – in a studio and group fitness setting – is contagious and inspires Members to reach their fitness goals…TOGETHER!!!

About Our Studio and Founder

On May 11, 2013, Let’s Do This (LDT) Fitness was founded by fitness instructor, Keith Williams, in Denver, Colorado. Initially, LDT Fitness offered only Zumba 3-Days a week. Then, Members requested more variety because their fitness began to plateau. LDT Fitness added LesMills programs: BodyPump and GRIT to its schedule and Members loved it! As the fitness industry became more competitive and technology more prevalent, LDT Fitness decided to embrace technology and become the pioneer of Virtual Fitness! LDT Fitness is still a privately owned “In-Studio” Virtual and Live group fitness facility. The Vision: Offer “In-Studio” Virtual and Live group fitness classes that were convenient and utilized innovative and cutting edge technology that motivated Members to workout…TOGETHER! Who knew you could take your favorite group fitness programs, with Member and friends-on your schedule? Now you can at LDT Fitness! Our motto: Let’s Do This…Together!!!
